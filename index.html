Litti Chokha
₹250

Litti Chokha is a traditional Bihari dish featuring roasted wheat balls (litti) stuffed with spiced gram flour, served with mashed vegetables (chokha) like brinjal, tomatoes, and potatoes, seasoned with mustard oil.

Chole Bhature
₹150

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish featuring spicy, tangy chickpea curry (chole) served with fluffy, deep-fried bread (bhature). It's rich, flavorful, and best enjoyed with pickles and onions.

Vada Pav
₹200

Vada pav is a popular Indian street food from Mumbai, featuring a spicy, deep-fried potato fritter (vada) sandwiched in a soft bun (pav), often served with chutneys and fried green chili.

Canadian Pizza
₹300

Pizza is a savory dish featuring a baked flatbread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. It’s versatile, flavorful, and loved worldwide.

Veggie Burger

A burger is a savory sandwich with a juicy beef patty, often layered with lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, onions, and sauces, all packed between soft buns for a delicious, handheld meal.

Malai Kofta
₹340

Malai Kofta is a rich, creamy North Indian curry made with soft, deep-fried vegetable or paneer balls ("koftas") simmered in a smooth tomato-based gravy, spiced with aromatic Indian flavors.

About us

Welcome to Foodie, your gateway to authentic Indian cuisine! Nestled in the heart of bihar, we offer a vibrant menu featuring classic dishes like aromatic biryanis, rich curries, and flavorful tandoori specialties. Each dish is crafted with the finest spices and ingredients, bringing the essence of India to your plate. Our warm, inviting ambiance is perfect for family gatherings, romantic dinners, or celebrations with friends. Enjoy our selection of traditional Indian beverages, from refreshing lassis to exotic chai. With attentive service and a passion for hospitality, foodie promises a dining experience that delights the senses and leaves you craving more. Join us for a culinary journey that celebrates the rich diversity of Indian flavors!
